Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What accounting services does Hlakaniphilez offer?

We offer bookkeeping, management reporting, financial statements, payroll processing, income tax, VAT and statutory submissions, and business compliance support — for both SMEs and individuals.

Do you assist with VAT and SARS submissions?

Yes. We handle VAT registration, monthly and bi-monthly VAT submissions, and all statutory submissions required by SARS for businesses and individuals.

Do you work with clients outside of Kloof / KZN?

Absolutely. We support clients across South Africa. Most of our work is handled remotely and electronically, so location is not a barrier to great service.
